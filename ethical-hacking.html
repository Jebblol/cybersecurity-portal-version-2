Ethical Hacking & Legal Boundaries

Ethical hackers help organizations find weaknesses before criminals do. The key difference is permission and responsible behavior.

What Ethical Hackers Do

  • Get written permission to test a system.
  • Scan for known vulnerabilities and misconfigurations.
  • Report findings clearly so they can be fixed.
  • Follow laws and non-disclosure agreements.

White Hat vs Black Hat vs Grey Hat

  • White hat: Authorized, legal, helps organizations.
  • Black hat: Illegal hacking for profit or damage.
  • Grey hat: No permission, but claims “good intentions” – still risky and often illegal.
