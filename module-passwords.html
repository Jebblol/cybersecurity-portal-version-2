Module 1: Passwords & Authentication

Weak passwords are the easiest way to get hacked. Strong passphrases and multi-factor authentication (MFA) make attacks much harder.

Key Concepts

  • Use long passphrases (12+ characters).
  • Mix of upper/lowercase, numbers, and symbols.
  • Never reuse passwords across important accounts.
  • Enable MFA for email, banking, and social media.
