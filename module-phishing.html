Module 2: Phishing & Social Engineering

Phishing messages try to trick you into clicking malicious links or giving away passwords. Learn to spot the red flags before you get scammed.

Key Concepts

  • Check the sender address, not just the display name.
  • Beware of urgent tone: “Your account will be closed in 24 hours!”.
  • Don’t click unexpected links or attachments.
  • When in doubt, go directly to the official website or app.
